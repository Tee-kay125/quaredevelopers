ABOUT Quare Developers


Quare Developers is an organisation that introduces technology to the community. Progammers from different provinces teaches the community how to write computer softwares . Quare Developers collaborate in learning, seeking new knowledge and constantly acquiring new skills alongside community members. We set a vision for creating learning experiences that provide the right tools and supports for all children to thrive

One Goal

We sets a nation vision and plan for learning enabled by technology through workshops. Children will learn useful life skills through technology

Why is technology important in the society?

The implementation of technology in our society helps close that gap. Technology has the ability to enhance relationships between parents and children. Technology helps make teaching and learning more meaningful and fun. Children are also able to collaborate with their own friends through technological applications.
